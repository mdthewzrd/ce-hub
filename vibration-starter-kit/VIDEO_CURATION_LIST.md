## **Video Selection Criteria**

### Must Have:
✓ Clear audio and video quality
✓ Under 15 minutes (ideally under 12)
✓ Accurate information
✓ Professional presentation
✓ Engaging but not overly sensational

### Prefer:
✓ Subtitles or captions available
✓ Under 100K views (up-and-coming creators)
✓ Recent uploads (within 3 years)
✓ Active channel with community

### Avoid:
✗ Outrageous claims without evidence
✗ Poor audio quality
✗ Overly long videos (20+ minutes)
✗ Clickbait titles
✗ Controversial/polarizing content

## **Embed Code Template**

```html
<div class="video-container">
    <iframe
        width="560"
        height="315"
        src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/VIDEO_ID"
        title="Video title"
        frameborder="0"
        allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture"
        allowfullscreen>
    </iframe>
</div>
```

Replace `VIDEO_ID` with the actual YouTube video ID (the part after `v=` in the URL).
