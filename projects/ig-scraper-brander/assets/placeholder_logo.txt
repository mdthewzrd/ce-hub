# Place your logo here!

## Logo Requirements

1. **Format**: PNG with transparency (recommended)
2. **Size**: 512x512px or higher (will be scaled down)
3. **Design**: Simple, readable at small sizes
4. **File name**: your_logo.png

## Example:

Create a simple logo using any image editor (Canva, Photoshop, etc.):
- Transparent background
- Your brand name or symbol
- High contrast for visibility

## Save as:

assets/your_logo.png
