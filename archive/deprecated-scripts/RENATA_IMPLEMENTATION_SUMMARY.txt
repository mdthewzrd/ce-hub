KEY DESIGN PRINCIPLES:
======================

1. Yellow Everywhere: Primary brand color used consistently
2. Dark & Minimal: Black backgrounds, minimal clutter
3. High Contrast: White text on dark backgrounds
4. Type-Based Styling: Different colored badges for message types
5. Multi-Personality: Easy mode switching with color indicators
6. Live Feedback: Visual status indicators for connection
7. Responsive: Sidebar or floating window both work
8. Smooth Animations: Transitions and hover states throughout
