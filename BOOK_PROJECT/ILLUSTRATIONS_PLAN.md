## STYLE GUIDELINES FOR ALL ILLUSTRATIONS

### Color Palette:
- **Primary:** Deep blue (wisdom), gold (enlightenment), white (clarity)
- **Secondary:** Green (nature/health), violet (spiritual), orange (energy)
- **Background:** Dark for energy visualizations, light for diagrams

### Typography:
- Headings: Sans-serif, modern (Inter, Roboto, or similar)
- Body text: Clean, readable (same as book body)
- Labels: Clear, concise, well-positioned

### Overall Aesthetic:
- Modern yet timeless
- Scientific yet accessible
- Precise yet artistic
- Informative yet beautiful

## TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

### Image Formats:
- **Vector:** SVG, AI, or EPS for diagrams and schematics
- **Raster:** PNG or TIFF for photographic elements
- **Resolution:** Minimum 300 DPI for print

### Dimensions:
- Full page: 8.5" x 11" (with margins)
- Half page: 8.5" x 5.5"
- Quarter page: 4.25" x 5.5"
- Spot illustrations: Variable
