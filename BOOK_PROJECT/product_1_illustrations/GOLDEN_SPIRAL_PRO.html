The Golden Spiral

Nature's Perfect Proportion

The Golden Spiral is a logarithmic spiral that grows outward by a factor of the golden ratio φ (approximately 1.618) for every quarter turn it makes. It appears throughout nature, from the arrangement of leaves on a stem to the spiral arms of galaxies, embodying the mathematical relationship that has fascinated artists, architects, and mathematicians for millennia.
