## THE DOUBLING CIRCUIT: Nature's Expansion Pattern

Now watch what happens when you double each number:

**1 × 2 = 2**
**2 × 2 = 4**
**4 × 2 = 8**
**8 × 2 = 16 → 1 + 6 = 7**
**16 × 2 = 32 → 3 + 2 = 5**
**32 × 2 = 64 → 6 + 4 = 10 → 1 + 0 = 1**

**See the pattern?**

1 → 2 → 4 → 8 → 7 → 5 → **1**

It loops back.

**Six numbers in a repeating sequence.**

**This is called the DOUBLING CIRCUIT.**

And here's why it matters:

**Doubling is the pattern of expansion in nature.**

- One cell becomes two
- Two become four
- Four become eight
- Life grows by doubling

**This circuit is the code of expansion.**
