## SPECIAL NOTES

**This is a SHAREABLE ASSET - designed specifically for Instagram.**

**Design Philosophy:**
- **Minimal over maximal:** Clean, focused, powerful
- **High contrast:** Gold on dark space = instantly readable
- **Center-aligned:** Creates focus and impact
- **Generous white space:** Let the quote breathe
- **Subtle sacred geometry:** Background pattern, not foreground

**Typography Strategy:**
- Cormorant Garamond Italic for main quote (elegant, mystical)
- Large font size (32pt+) for impact
- Intentional line breaks for dramatic pauses
- Center alignment for Instagram feed appeal

**Instagram Optimization:**
- 1080x1080 square (standard feed)
- Alt: 1080x1350 vertical (4:5, takes more screen space)
- Test in dark mode AND light mode
- Leave room for Instagram UI (likes, comments)
- Avoid putting text too close to edges

**Background Pattern:**
- Very subtle Flower of Life or Seed of Life
- 5% opacity maximum
- Should be barely visible
- Adds depth without distraction
- Reinforces brand identity

**Call to Action:**
- No explicit CTA needed in the graphic itself
- The quote IS the hook
- Caption will handle CTA
- Focus on shareability

**Brand Alignment:**
- **Ancient wisdom:** Mystical quote style
- **Modern precision:** Clean, professional design
- **Mystical but grounded:** Not woo-woo, just profound

**A/B Testing Note:**
Create 2-3 variations of quote graphics with different quotes to test which performs best on Instagram.
