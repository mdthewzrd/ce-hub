## SPECIAL NOTES

**The Vesica Piscis is the BUILIDING BLOCK of sacred geometry.**

**What is Vesica Piscis:**
- Latin for "bladder of the fish"
- Two circles overlapping
- Each center on other's circumference
- Creates lens or "fish" shape
- Foundation for ALL sacred geometry

**Historical & Religious Significance:**
- Christian symbol: Fish (Ichthys)
- Used in cathedral architecture (doorways, arches)
- Represents divine feminine (birth portal)
- Portal between worlds
- Found in ancient art worldwide

**Geometric Properties:**
- FIRST shape created from Seed of Life
- Contains √2, √3, and φ
- Forms equilateral triangle
- Creates 60° and 30° angles
- Basis for Golden Rectangle construction

**Construction:**
1. Draw first circle
2. Place compass on any point on circumference
3. Draw second circle
4. Creates Vesica Piscis overlap
5. Continue process → Seed of Life

**Why It Matters:**
This is the SIMPLEST sacred geometry shape, but it contains the mathematics for ALL other shapes. It's the "seed" of the entire system.
