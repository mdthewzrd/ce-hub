## Tesla's Vision: Longitudinal Waves and Scalar Energy

Nikola Tesla discovered that electromagnetic energy could propagate as:
- **Transverse waves** (what conventional physics uses)
- **Longitudinal waves** (Tesla's discovery)

Longitudinal waves are like sound waves—compressions and rarefactions in the medium rather than oscillations perpendicular to direction of travel.

## Coherence and Phase Conjugation

### What is Coherence?

**Coherence** means waves are:
- In phase with each other
- Organized rather than random
- Capable of constructive interference
- Able to maintain their structure over time

Examples:
- **Laser light** is coherent (all waves in sync)
- **Light bulb** light is incoherent (random phases)
- **Coherent systems** can do things incoherent ones cannot

### Phase Conjugation

**Phase conjugation** is a phenomenon where:
- A wave is reflected back along its exact path
- The wavefront is reversed in time
- The wave automatically retraces its path

This creates:
- **Self-correcting beams** that maintain coherence
- **Non-linear optical effects** that can amplify signals
- **Perfect reflection** regardless of distortions

In unified field technologies:
