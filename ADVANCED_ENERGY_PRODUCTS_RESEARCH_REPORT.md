### Legal & Compliance Requirements

**FDA Regulations**:
- **Medical Device Classification**: Class II/III devices require FDA clearance
- **510(k) Premarket Notification**: Required for most Class II devices
- **Establishment Registration & Device Listing**: Mandatory
- **Quality System Regulations (QSR)**: Manufacturing compliance required
- **Medical Device Reporting (MDR)**: Adverse event reporting
- **Labeling Requirements**: Strict restrictions on medical claims

**General Wellness Policy**:
- FDA provides guidance for low-risk wellness products
- Distinguishes between medical claims vs. general wellness claims
- Products avoiding medical claims may not require FDA clearance

**Medical Claim Restrictions**:
- Cannot claim to treat, diagnose, cure specific diseases
- Allowed: "promotes relaxation," "supports wellness," "energy balancing"
- Prohibited: "treats arthritis," "cures chronic pain," "diagnoses conditions"

**Platform Policies**:
- **Amazon**: Prohibits Class II/III medical devices without FDA authorization documentation
- **Etsy**: More lenient, treats as handmade wellness items
- **Own Website**: Full control but full liability

## 7. LEGAL & COMPLIANCE - DETAILED ANALYSIS

### FDA Regulations Overview

**Medical Device Classifications**:
- **Class I**: Low risk (general controls)
- **Class II**: Moderate risk (special controls + 510(k) clearance)
- **Class III**: High risk (premarket approval)

**PEMF Device Status**:
- **Classification**: Class II/III medical devices
- **Product Code**: MBQ (peripheral electromagnetic field)
- **Clearance Required**: 510(k) premarket notification for most devices
- **Exemptions**: General wellness policy may apply for non-medical claims

### General Wellness Policy Guidance

The FDA provides compliance guidance for low-risk wellness products promoting healthy lifestyles.

**Allowed (General Wellness)**:
- Promotes relaxation
- Supports wellness
- Enhances energy
- Improves sleep quality
- Reduces stress

**Prohibited (Medical Claims)**:
- Treats arthritis
- Cures chronic pain
- Diagnoses conditions
- Prevents disease
- "Clinically proven" without FDA clearance

**Required Disclaimers**:
- "Not intended to diagnose, treat, cure or prevent any disease"
- "These statements have not been evaluated by the Food and Drug Administration"
- "This product is not intended to replace medical treatment"

### FTC Requirements

**Substantiation Requirement**:
- Must have competent and reliable scientific evidence
- Evidence must support specific claims made
- Testimonials must be authentic and representative
- Before/after photos must be genuine

**Clear Disclosure Required**:
- Material connections (affiliate relationships, free products)
- Typical results vs. exceptional cases
- Paid endorsements must be disclosed

### Platform-Specific Policies

#### **Amazon**
**Medical Device Restrictions**:
- Class II and Class III medical devices prohibited without FDA authorization
- Prescription devices require licensed practitioner verification
- OTC devices must meet FDA recognition standards

**Enforcement**:
- Account suspensions possible
- Product listing removals
- Inventory stranded in FBA warehouses

**Strategy**:
- Avoid medical claims entirely
- Focus on wellness positioning
- Include all required disclaimers
- Consider avoiding Amazon for regulated products

#### **Etsy**
**More Lenient Approach**:
- Treats products as handmade wellness items
- Less strict medical device enforcement
- Still requires compliance with FTC guidelines

**Best For**:
- Crystal/orgone products
- Handmade energy devices
- Artisan wellness jewelry
- Non-medical positioning

#### **Shopify/Own Website**
**Full Control**:
- No product restrictions
- Complete brand messaging control
- Full liability responsibility

**Requirements**:
- Must still comply with FTC/FDA
- Platform doesn't provide protection
- Legal responsibility falls entirely on business

### Product Liability Insurance

**Recommended Coverage**:
- **General Liability**: $1-2 million coverage
- **Product Liability**: Included or separate
- **Annual Cost**: $500-3,000 depending on products and revenue

**Why Essential**:
- Protection from lawsuits

### Import/Export Considerations

**Import Regulations**:
- **Customs Declarations**: Accurate description and valuation required
- **Tariffs/Duties**: Vary by country and material (some crystals have CITES restrictions)
