üí° Quick Tagging Tips

Frame Style Detection

Look for key shape words in titles: "aviator", "cat eye", "round", "square", "rectangle", "wayfarer". Check metafields too!

Material Keywords

Wire/metal = "wire", "metal", "gold", "silver", "thin". Acetate = "acetate", "plastic", "tortoise", "thick frame".

Lens Types

Only tag if explicitly mentioned. "Polarized", "RX" or "prescription", "blue light", "tinted", "photochromic".

When Uncertain

Use the visual shape as your primary guide. When in doubt, leave it blank for manual review rather than guessing.
