# 🧭 Traderra — Professional Adaptive Edition
### Product Requirements Document (v1.0)
*Codename: “Renata” AI System*

---

## 1. Executive Summary

**Traderra** is a professional trading journal and analytics platform built for serious traders.  
It merges **data transparency**, **AI-assisted reflection**, and **immersive, cinematic design** into a single unified workspace.  

At its core, Traderra’s mission is to create a **truthful trading environment** — a space where ego is stripped away and performance is measured objectively, guided by a professional-grade AI assistant: **Renata**.

> “Traderra doesn’t coddle traders. It shows them the truth — beautifully.”

---

## 2. Vision & Product Philosophy

### Mission Statement
To give traders a clear, elegant environment where every decision, every stat, and every reflection builds toward consistent, data-driven performance.

- **Adaptive professionalism.**
- **Total immersion.**

## 8. Data Architecture

- **Frontend:** React + CopilotKit + AGUI  
- **Backend:** FastAPI / Next.js  
- **Database:** PostgreSQL + pgvector  
- **Storage:** R2 / S3  
- **Cache:** Redis  
- **Auth:** Clerk  
- **Billing:** Stripe  
- **Market Data:** Polygon API

## Appendix A – AI Interaction Examples

### Analyst Mode
> **Renata:** “Your win rate this week fell 12% compared to last. Expectancy dropped from 0.85R to 0.46R. You deviated from your entry timing rule in 4 trades — shall we analyze those first?”

### Coach Mode
> **Renata:** “This week was challenging, but there’s progress. You maintained your average R per winner, even with a dip in win %. Let’s review setups with lower consistency.”

### Mentor Mode
> **Renata:** “You’ve shown discipline in execution even when outcomes were less favorable. Remember — edge reveals itself over time, not per trade. Let’s look at where patience paid off.”
